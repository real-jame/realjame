Watch

Wanna cut down on phone usage? Try a smartwatch!

Buy an expensive companion for your phone, and that’ll lead you to use your phone less? It sounds like an oxymoron. But after over a month of using my Google Pixel Watch, it seems to be true! So, yeah. I’ve been dealing with spending more time on my phone than I’d like. And I try stuff like time tracking apps, blocking, etc. but nothing works. Quite simply, if my phone was there, I would be on it, such as before going to bed and then immediately waking up.
