🔗 James Somers: 'More people should write' (2012)

That’s the promise: you will live more curiously if you write. You will become a scientist, if not of the natural world than of whatever world you care about. More of that world will pop alive. You will see more when you look at it. Read it: https://jsomers.net/blog/more-people-should-write Fantastic, inspiring post. I’ve kept journals the whole year because of this, and I won’t stop. Writing, being able to format and output thoughts in your mind, is one of the most effective tools for better valuing and reflecting on each day that goes by.
